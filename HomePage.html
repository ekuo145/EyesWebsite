The eye is the sensory organ that we see out of.


It is one of the most important parts of the body, pretty much all animals have it.


Being responsible for vision, it is crucial that we take care of our eyes.


The following material will ensure you have an understanding of the eye and how it works, and what some common defects are.

Structure of the eye


The eye is a complex organ composed of many different parts that work together to enable us to see.

Key components include the cornea, iris, lens, retina, and optic nerve (made up of rods and cones).

Common Issues with Vision


Over 60% adults in the United States wear glasses.

These can be due to a variety of issues, but it is mainly due to age related farsightedness.

There are a variety of other reasons as well, as many people suffer from nearsightedness and colorblindness.
